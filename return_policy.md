# Return & Refund Policy
**Gittens Mercantile** | Effective Date: January 1, 2026

---

## Overview

At Gittens Mercantile, we want you to love what you ordered. If something isn't right, we're here to make it easy. This policy applies to all purchases made on our website, mobile app, and in-store locations.

---

## Standard Return Window

Most items can be returned within **30 days** of the delivery date for a full refund to your original payment method. Items must be:

- Unused and in their original condition
- In original packaging with all tags attached
- Accompanied by a receipt or proof of purchase

---

## Extended Return Window

The following categories qualify for a **60-day return window**:

- Furniture and large home décor
- Bedding and mattress toppers (unopened only)
- Seasonal and holiday items purchased more than 45 days before the relevant holiday

---

## Non-Returnable Items

The following items are **final sale** and cannot be returned or exchanged:

- Opened mattresses
- Custom or personalized items
- Gift cards
- Clearance items marked "Final Sale"
- Items damaged due to misuse or improper care

---

## Refund Processing

- **Credit and debit cards:** Refunds are issued within 5–7 business days of receiving the returned item
- **Store credit:** Issued immediately upon return approval
- **Original shipping charges:** Non-refundable unless the return is due to a Gittens Mercantile error (wrong item, damaged in transit, etc.)

---

## Exchanges

We offer direct exchanges for the same item in a different size or color, subject to availability. If the desired item is out of stock, we will issue store credit for the full purchase amount.

---

## Damaged or Defective Items

If your item arrives damaged or defective, contact us within **7 days** of delivery. We will arrange a free return shipping label and send a replacement at no additional cost. Photo documentation of the damage may be required.

---

## How to Start a Return

1. Visit [gittensmercantile.com/returns](http://gittensmercantile.com/returns) or use our mobile app
2. Enter your order number and email address
3. Select the item(s) you wish to return and the reason
4. Print your prepaid return shipping label (domestic orders only)
5. Drop off the package at any authorized carrier location within **7 days** of label generation

---

## Return Shipping Costs

- **Domestic returns:** Free prepaid label for orders over $75. A $7.95 return shipping fee applies to orders under $75, deducted from your refund.
- **International returns:** Customer is responsible for return shipping costs. We recommend using a trackable shipping method.

---

## In-Store Returns

Items purchased online may be returned to any Gittens Mercantile retail location within the applicable return window. Bring your packing slip or confirmation email. In-store credit is issued immediately; refunds to the original payment method may take 3–5 business days.
